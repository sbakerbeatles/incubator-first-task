Stephen's Success Story

I envision that after completing the Incubator and the working groups, I will have the skills and the confidence to be successful as a Junior Software Engineer. Being in an environment where everyone is on the same level is beneficial because we are all learning the same thing. In addition to that, nobody would feel as if they are the dumb one in the class. I hope to gain experience in software development and make the transition into devops.
